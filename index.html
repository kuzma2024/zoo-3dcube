How to Play

Getting started with Zoo 3D Cube is simple and straightforward. Use your mouse or touch controls to rotate and manipulate the 3D cubes, solving puzzles by arranging them in the correct configuration. Each level introduces new mechanics and challenges, keeping the gameplay fresh and engaging.

The game automatically saves your progress, so you can pick up where you left off at any time. Challenge yourself to complete levels with the minimum number of moves, or simply enjoy the relaxing puzzle-solving experience at your own pace.
